The Indian Penal Code (IPC) is the official criminal code of India. It is a comprehensive code intended to cover all substantive aspects of criminal law.
Section 378 - Theft: Whoever, intending to take dishonestly any moveable property out of the possession of any person without that person's consent, moves that property in order to such taking, is said to commit theft.
Section 300 - Murder: Except in the cases hereinafter excepted, culpable homicide is murder.
Section 420 - Cheating: Cheating and dishonestly inducing delivery of property.
